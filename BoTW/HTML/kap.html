Welcome to The Kaptivators artist page

The Kaptivators are a Manchester based collective who live and breath funk and rhythm. Their sound is an eclectic mix of Hip-Hop, Soul, Dub and Electro. Uplifting themes and social commentary form part of their story. Born through a love of live music, they came together to make and share music for the soul, living their lives the only way they know.
